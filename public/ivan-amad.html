Art.

Craft.

I'm Ivan Amad, a visual art director and multidisciplinary designer creating concept art, branding systems, social media visuals, booth designs, storyboards, logos, and pixel art. My work blends cinematic composition, strong visual identity, and experimental digital aesthetics.
